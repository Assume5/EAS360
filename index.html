How To Make An Aerodynamic Paper Airplane?

Introduction:

In this guide we will teach you how to make an aerodynamic paper airplane. These airplanes can be used for entertainment by throwing them around and watching them fly through the air.

Conditions:

1. We need a flat surface desk for the best result
2. Need a piece of 8 ½” by 11” paper to perform a paper airplane
3. Around 10 mins to build a paper airplane
4. Markers to customize your paper airplane

Hazards:

Paper cut:

1. Preventing paper cut

  • Moisturize your hands.
  • Wearing gloves
  • Pick the paper up slowly

2. Deal with paper cut

  • If you get paper cut, make sure you use alcohol disinfection.
  • After using alcohol disinfection, use a bind aid.

Step 1)

Place sheet of paper on a flat surface

1) Find any type of rectangular paper

2) Find any type of rectangular paper

Step 2)

Fold paper in half lengthwise

1) Make sure to fold paper in half so that the edges are aligned

If not folded symmetrically you plane’s weight distribution will be off

2) Once Folded run your fingers down the fold to create a defined crease

Step 3)

Unfold the paper

1) Unfold the paper and if step 2 was followed you should see a defined crease down the center of the paper

If no crease is visible repeat step 2

Step 4)

Fold the two corners on the top of the paper into the crease.

1) Fold the two top corners of the paper to the middle crease.

2) Make sure to crease each of the newly folded corners so the paper maintains its shape as shown in the image.

Step 5)

Fold the angled edges into the crease

1) Fold the newly folded bottom corners from step 4 to the middle crease to ensure a more aerodynamic design.

For an easier less aerodynamic design skip this step and move onto step 6

If Step 5 is skipped, the final plane will not look like the finished project outlined in this guide

Step 6)

Fold the paper in half along the centerline

1) Fold the paper in half so that the previous folds are on the inside of the new fold.

If fold is made so that the folds from step 5 are on the outside the plane will loss shape and likely not fly properly

Step 7)

Fold the angled edges toward the center fold to shape the wings

1) Fold each angled edge to the middle fold and crease them down to keep shape.

2) Take the creased wings and unfold them slightly such that they create a flat top as shown in the picture

Step 8)

Customize the plane as you see fit

1) Using any tools such as markers or pens customize your paper airplane to look how you please.

For example as shown in the image you can add a face to the nose of the plane to make it look cool.

Step 9)

Throw the plane into the air with the same form used for throwing a dart

1) Hold your plane with two fingers in between the middle and back of the plane body

2) Make sure you are holding the plane parallel to the ground.

3) Flick your wrist and let go of the plane and watch it fly

Do not throw your planes at other people or object it can result in injury of breaking things make sure you the area you are throwing is clear

After all, all you need to make a paper airplane is paper!. If you follow these steps, your airplane will fly much better than a simple design! If you want it to be stronger, you can use a light but thick piece of paper such as construction paper to increase the plane's durability. Don't forget step 8 because it will make it easier to distinguish which paper airplane is yours. Now you know how to make an aerodynamic paper airplane. Have fun.
